Chrome Extension

We use a chrome extension to automatically capture your schedule data and format it for the web panel. It takes less than 30 seconds to work its magic. This does not store any of your data.

  1. Download Extension
  2. Open new tab
  3. Click on the icon on the menu bar
  4. Click "Make My Schedule"
  5. Log in to MyBackpack
  6. Wait for the extension to redirect you
  7. Click on the icon on the menu bar
  8. Click "Make My Schedule"
  9. Use the web panel to make any changes you want
